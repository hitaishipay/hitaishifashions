Help & FAQs

How do I track my order?

You can track your order using the "Track Order" link in the top header of our website. Simply enter your order ID to get the latest updates.

What is your return policy?

We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy on most items. Please visit our "Returns & Exchanges" section in the footer for detailed information.

How can I change my shipping address?

If your order has not yet shipped, you might be able to change your address. Please contact customer support immediately for assistance.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we only ship within India. We are working to expand our shipping services to other countries soon.

How do I apply a discount code?

You can apply your discount code during the checkout process. Look for the "Apply Discount Code" field before finalizing your purchase.
